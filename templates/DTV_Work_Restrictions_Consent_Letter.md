## 1. CONFIRMATION OF UNDERSTANDING OF DTV WORK RESTRICTIONS

I hereby confirm and acknowledge that I FULLY UNDERSTAND and ACCEPT the work restrictions associated with the Destination Thailand Visa (DTV), specifically:

• I understand that the DTV visa permits me to work REMOTELY for employers, clients, or businesses located OUTSIDE of Thailand only
• I understand that I am PROHIBITED from engaging in any form of employment with Thai employers or Thai-registered companies
• I understand that I am PROHIBITED from conducting business activities that generate income within Thailand or from Thai sources
• I understand that I am PROHIBITED from taking up any position, role, or employment that would normally be filled by a Thai national or require a Thai work permit

• I have NO intention of conducting business activities that generate income within Thailand
• I will NOT engage in any work that requires a Thai work permit or would violate Thai labor laws
